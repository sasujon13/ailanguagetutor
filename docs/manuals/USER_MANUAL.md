# AI Language Tutor — User Manual

Welcome to **Cheradip AI Language Tutor**. This guide explains every part of the app for learners, whether you are on a free trial, Pro, or Plus plan.

---

## 1. What this app does

AI Language Tutor helps you:

- **Scan** books, worksheets, and notes (camera or gallery)
- **Read** scanned text with tap-to-define words and grammar hints
- **Practice** speaking, typing, and listening in up to 3 languages at once
- **Study grammar** with an AI-generated grammar book per language
- **Work offline** using downloaded language packs (meanings and pronunciation)

The app is **offline-first**: dictionary meanings come from packs on your phone. AI explanations use the cloud or your home server when online.

---

## 2. First launch & trial

1. Complete **Onboarding** — pick study languages (up to 3), voice gender, and app language.
2. You receive a **30-day trial** with full Pro features (AI modes 1–4, scanner, reader, practice).
3. After the trial, subscribe on the **Paywall** or continue with offline packs only.

**Guest use (not signed in):** You can browse and use up to **99 free AI requests**. After that, sign in or subscribe to continue AI features.

## 4. Home — Scan Only vs Learning

On Home, choose your activity mode:

### Scan Only
- Only the **Scan** action is shown.
- After scanning, tap **Save** to export PDF/images — no OCR or reader flow.
- Useful for archiving documents without processing.

### Learning (default)
- All actions: Scan, Camera, Import, Practice, Type, Voice, Listen, History, Grammar.
- **Scan / Camera / Import** → OCR → structured reader.
- **Practice / Type / Voice** → Practice hub.

**Example:** Scan a French worksheet → Process & Read → tap words for definitions and grammar.

---

## 5. Scanning documents

1. Tap **Scan** (or Camera / Import from Home).
2. Capture one or more pages; use the editor to crop, rotate, and adjust contrast.
3. Thumbnails appear below the large preview — tap to switch pages.
4. Choose:
   - **Process & Read** — runs OCR and opens the reader (Learning mode).
   - **Save** — export only (Scan Only mode).

**Tips:**
- Good lighting and flat pages improve OCR accuracy.
- Math, code, and diagrams may take longer — the app routes them to cloud structuring when online.

---

## 6. Reader

After OCR processing, the **Reader** shows structured text (headings, code blocks, paragraphs).

- **Tap any word** — definition sheet from your offline pack + optional AI grammar explanation.
- **Grammar depth** (Settings) controls how detailed AI hints are: word, sentence, or paragraph.
- Documents appear under **Learning → History**.

---

## 7. Practice hub

Open from Home → **Practice**, **Type**, or **Voice**.

1. Pick **input language** and **output language** (from your active packs).
2. Choose **Answer** (tutor explains) or **Translation** (direct translation).
3. Choose an **AI mode** (Settings → AI Mode Selection):
   - **Smart Tutor** — explanations (recommended)
   - **Fast Translation** — NLLB translation
   - **Balanced** — translation + cleanup
   - **High Accuracy** — Plus only; best for difficult texts

**Voice:** Hold the mic, speak, release. Calibrate once per language for better recognition.

**Typed input:** Type a sentence or question; tap words in input or AI response for grammar help.

---

## 8. Grammar book

Menu ☰ → **Grammar** (or Home → Grammar).

- Select a language you have downloaded.
- Browse chapters generated for that language (cached after first load).
- Works offline with cached content; refreshes when online.

---

## 9. Languages & offline packs

**Languages** tab (☰ menu):

1. Search and download language packs (meanings + TTS data).
2. Activate up to **3 languages** at once — these appear in Practice and app language options.
3. Packs work **fully offline** for dictionary lookups.

**Example:** Download Spanish and English → set both active → scan a Spanish page → tap "hablar" for the English meaning offline.

## 10. Subscriptions & AI modes

| Plan | Price (launch) | AI access |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| **Free** | After 30-day trial | Offline packs only |
| **Pro** | $1/mo · $10/yr | AI modes 1–4 |
| **Plus** | $3/mo · $30/yr | All Pro modes + **Mode 5 High Accuracy** |

**Mode 4 (Lightweight)** applies automatically when you scan (OCR cleanup) — you do not pick it manually.

**Mode 5 (High Accuracy)** uses the largest AI model and is **Plus only**. Pro users see a lock icon and upgrade prompt.

Manage your plan: **Profile → Subscription** or ☰ → Subscription.

## 13. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Try this |
|---------|----------|
| OCR text looks messy | Ensure good lighting; use Process & Read while online for math/code |
| AI says offline | Check Wi‑Fi; verify subscription or guest limit (99 requests) |
| No word definitions | Download the language pack on Languages tab |
| Mode 5 locked | Upgrade to Plus on Subscription screen |
| Trial expired | Subscribe Pro or Plus; offline packs still work |

---

## 14. Privacy & data

- Scanned documents stay on your device unless you export them.
- Login syncs referrals and subscription status with Cheradip servers.
- AI requests send text to the configured backend (cloud or home AI) for processing.

For support, contact the address shown on the Paywall or your referral dashboard.
